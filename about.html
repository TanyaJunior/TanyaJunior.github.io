About Me

Tanya

Hey, I'm Tanya Chisati! I specialize in front-end development and have dedicated a significant amount of time to working on side projects using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I'm passionate about creating engaging user interfaces and bringing designs to life through coding. Currently, I reside in Cape Town. Although I'm now based here, I originally grew up in Johannesburg, South Africa, and I have strong roots in Zimbabwe.
The combination of these diverse experiences has enriched my perspective and helped shape my approach to different cultures. I'm excited to continue honing my front-end skills, exploring new technologies, and contributing to meaningful projects that make a positive impact.
